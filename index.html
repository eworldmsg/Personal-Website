About Me

Dr. Edward W. Savarese is a pioneer in the computer industry, successfully building a $50M company in the digital marketplace. With decades of experience as an Entrepreneur, Executive, and Educator, he has led multiple successful ventures, including founding 2 Public Companies, ImageTech Corporation (Formerly, Personal Computer Products, Inc.) and Com-Guard.com, Inc. Dr. Savarese holds a Doctoral Degree from Columbia University, Teachers College in the Department of Curriculum and Computing and has a rich history of leadership and teaching.
